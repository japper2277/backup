Current Critical Problems

Major UX issues preventing the calendar from being a seamless companion to your map

Poor Integration

Calendar feels like a completely separate app. No shared state, inconsistent styling, and confusing navigation between map and calendar views.

Inconsistent Design System

Different colors, fonts, spacing, and component styles from the main app. Breaks user mental model and feels unprofessional.

Clunky Mobile Experience

No mobile navigation bar, poor touch targets, and difficult navigation. Doesn't feel native on mobile devices.

Inefficient Add-from-Map Flow

Complex multi-step process to add mics from map to calendar. Should be one-click operation with smart pre-filling.
